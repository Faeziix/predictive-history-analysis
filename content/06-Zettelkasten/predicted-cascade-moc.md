# The Predicted Cascade

Professor Jiang's most ambitious claim: current events follow a predictable cascade from an initial trigger (US-Iran war) through economic collapse, social upheaval, and civilizational restructuring. Each stage triggers the next.

## Phase 1: The Trigger
**US-Iran War** ([[CLAIM-001-us-iran-war]])

The catalyst. The US attacks Iran, expecting a quick victory. Instead, it encounters the [[law-of-asymmetry]] - Iran's terrain, asymmetric capabilities, and societal cohesion make it a quagmire worse than Iraq or Afghanistan.

## Phase 2: The Chokepoint
**Strait of Hormuz Closure** ([[CLAIM-002-strait-of-hormuz]])

Iran's primary asymmetric weapon. Closing the strait cuts ~20% of global oil transit. This isn't just a military event - it's an economic trigger that activates the [[price-hierarchy]] cascade.

## Phase 3: Economic Shock
**Global Economy Collapse** ([[CLAIM-006-global-economy-collapse]])

Oil price spike -> fertilizer production collapses (petrochemical dependent) -> food systems fail. The [[cost-pyramid]] inverts: the cheap energy that enabled globalization disappears, and the entire structure built on it crumbles.

## Phase 4: Domestic Fracture
**National Draft and Civil Unrest** ([[CLAIM-003-national-draft-civil-war]])

The failing war demands more troops. A draft activates the [[law-of-proximity]] - Americans care more about their own lives than a distant war. Combined with [[elite-overproduction]], this triggers domestic upheaval.

## Phase 5: Financial Restructuring
**Petrodollar Collapse** ([[CLAIM-010-gcc-destruction-petrodollar]])

The dollar's reserve status depends on oil being traded in dollars. When the oil system breaks, so does the financial system. [[engineered-boom-bust-cycles|Boom-bust dynamics]] reach their terminal phase.

## Phase 6: Regional Fragmentation
**Mercantile Blocs** ([[CLAIM-012-regional-mercantilism]])

Globalization dies. Nations retreat into self-sufficient blocs. The [[technate]] (North America), [[third-rome-concept]] (Russia), [[pax-judaica-concept]] (Israel-centered), and others compete.

## Phase 7: Eschatological Climax
**Third Temple and Religious Convergence** ([[CLAIM-005-al-aqsa-third-temple]])

The [[law-of-eschatological-convergence]] reaches its peak. Religious narratives from multiple civilizations converge on Jerusalem, making prophecy self-fulfilling.

## Phase 8: New World Order
**Competing Civilization-States** ([[CLAIM-007-pax-judaica]], [[CLAIM-004-russia-third-rome]], [[CLAIM-008-trump-technate]])

The final state: the liberal international order is dead. In its place, civilization-states organized around religion, manufacturing capacity, and surveillance technology. [[CLAIM-015-depopulation-resource-wars|Depopulation]] reshapes demographics. [[CLAIM-011-israel-absorbs-gcc|Regional absorption]] reshapes borders.

## The Logic

Each phase makes the next more likely. This is the core insight of the [[universal-law-of-game-theory]] applied to geopolitics: **once the first domino falls, the cascade is structural, not contingent**. Individual actors can't stop it because each is rationally responding to the conditions created by the previous phase.

Whether you find this convincing depends on whether you accept the initial premises - particularly the [[law-of-asymmetry]] applied to US-Iran and the [[price-hierarchy]] analysis of oil dependency.
