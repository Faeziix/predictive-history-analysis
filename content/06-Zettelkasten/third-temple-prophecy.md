# Third Temple Prophecy

**Core idea:** The rebuilding of the Jewish Temple in Jerusalem - on the site currently occupied by the Al-Aqsa Mosque and Dome of the Rock - is the convergence point where multiple civilizational scripts collide. It is simultaneously the most sacred and most dangerous 35 acres on earth.

## The History

1. **First Temple (Solomon's Temple)** - Built ~957 BCE, destroyed by Babylonians 586 BCE
2. **Second Temple (Herod's Temple)** - Built ~516 BCE, destroyed by Romans 70 CE
3. **Third Temple** - Not yet built. The site is occupied by the Al-Aqsa Mosque (built ~705 CE)

## Why Every Tradition Cares

### Judaism
The Third Temple is necessary for the Messiah to come. [[chabad-lubavitch]] and the Temple Institute actively prepare for its construction. Red heifers have been imported for purification rituals.

### Christianity
[[christian-zionist-eschatology]] requires the Third Temple for the Second Coming. American evangelical support for Israel is driven by this theological requirement.

### Islam
Al-Aqsa is the third holiest site in Islam. Its destruction would be a universal casus belli - the one event that unites all Islamic factions (Sunni and Shia) into a single cause. See [[islamic-eschatology]].

## The Predicted Scenario

Jiang predicts ([[CLAIM-005-al-aqsa-third-temple]]):
1. During the fog of the US-Iran war, Al-Aqsa is destroyed
2. It's blamed on a stray Iranian missile (providing cover)
3. Israel clears the site and begins Third Temple construction
4. This unites the entire Muslim world against Israel
5. The prophesied War of Gog and Magog begins
6. [[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica]] is established through the crisis

The destruction serves *all three* eschatological traditions simultaneously:
- Jews get their Temple
- Christians get their Second Coming precondition
- Muslims get their unifying cause (which Shia eschatology predicts they ultimately win)

## Key Insight

**The Third Temple is where prophecy becomes policy.** It's not a theoretical religious belief - it's an active construction project with political backing, theological urgency, and military implications. The [[law-of-eschatological-convergence]] predicts that when multiple nuclear-armed civilizations want the same event (for different reasons), it happens.
