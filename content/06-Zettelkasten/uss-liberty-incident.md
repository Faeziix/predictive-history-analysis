# USS Liberty Incident

**Core idea:** In 1967, during the Six-Day War, Israeli forces intentionally attacked an unarmed American intelligence ship - the USS Liberty - in international waters, killing 34 Americans and wounding 171. The attack was covered up by the US government, and surviving sailors were ordered to stay silent. The incident that reveals the true nature of the "special relationship."

## What Happened

During the 1967 Six-Day War:
1. The USS Liberty, an American intelligence ship, was operating in international waters
2. Israeli forces strafed, torpedoed, and attempted to completely sink the ship
3. The goal: kill every American sailor on board to prevent them from reporting on Israeli war crimes
4. 34 Americans killed, 171 wounded

## The Cover-Up

President Lyndon B. Johnson covered up the attack. The official justification: he didn't want to escalate tensions with the Soviet Union by formally acknowledging Israel's intentional attack on an American vessel. The surviving sailors were ordered to stay silent.

The US government sold out its own servicemen to protect the reputation of a foreign state.
