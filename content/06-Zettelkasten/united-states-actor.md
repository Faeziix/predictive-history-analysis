# United States

The current global hegemon and the "muscle" enforcing the [[petrodollar-system]] and the "Rules-Based International Order." In Jiang's framework, the US is not a nation pursuing its interests - it's a host organism being consumed by [[transnational-capital]] parasites while simultaneously being deliberately crashed by its own president.

## Role in the Framework

The US serves two contradictory functions:

1. **The Empire.** The military and financial infrastructure that enforces the global [[price-hierarchy]]. CENTCOM protects oil trade routes. The dollar system extracts wealth globally. The [[military-industrial-complex]] converts tax revenue into private profit.

2. **The Target.** Under Trump's strategy ([[CLAIM-008-trump-technate]]), the empire itself must be destroyed to purge the parasites and rebuild as the [[technate]].

## The Internal War

The US is riven by [[elite-overproduction]] - two elite factions fighting to control the machinery of state:

### Old Elite ([[wall-street-actor]])
- Democrats, legacy media, academia
- Protect the existing financial extraction system
- Want the Iran war to fail so they can blame Trump and recapture power
- Need government bailouts for their $2 trillion private credit bubble

### Counter-Elite ([[silicon-valley-actor]])
- MAGA, tech oligarchs, AI industry
- Want to replace the old extraction system with a new one (AI surveillance)
- Want the Iran war to create emergency powers
- Need government bailouts for the AI bubble

Both factions support the [[CLAIM-001-us-iran-war|war]] for opposite domestic reasons. The [[law-of-proximity]] ensures the domestic game always overrides the foreign one.

## Military Weakness

Despite being the most expensive military in history, the US is structurally weak:

- **Inverse [[cost-pyramid]]:** Relies on exorbitantly expensive weapons (F-35, Gerald Ford carrier, THAAD) that are neutralized by cheap asymmetric weapons
- **[[military-industrial-complex]] corruption:** Weapons are designed to spend money, not win wars ([[CLAIM-014-mic-siphon]])
- **No manufacturing base:** Can't produce munitions for a sustained war of attrition
- **No political will:** The [[social-contract-vs-iron-and-blood|"Nation Game" model]] means commitment is transactional, not existential

## Predicted Fate

1. Loses the war against Iran ([[CLAIM-001-us-iran-war]])
2. Institutes a national draft ([[CLAIM-003-national-draft-civil-war]])
3. Draft triggers civil unrest, then civil war
4. Engineered economic collapse wipes out [[transnational-capital]]'s US positions
5. Retreats from global stage
6. Rebuilds as the [[technate]] - a self-sufficient North American manufacturing bloc under Christian Nationalism

## Key Insight

The US isn't declining accidentally. In Jiang's framework, it's being *deliberately crashed* from the inside (by Trump) and from above (by transnational capital moving to Israel). The question isn't whether the empire falls but which faction controls what comes after.
