# Silicon Valley (The Counter-Elite)

In the [[elite-overproduction]] framework, Silicon Valley represents the "counter-elite" - the new faction challenging the old [[wall-street-actor|Wall Street]] establishment for control of the American state.

## The Faction

- **Political alignment:** Republican/MAGA
- **Economic base:** AI, tech platforms, surveillance technology
- **Key figures:** Elon Musk, Peter Thiel, the Palantir ecosystem
- **Financial interest:** Government bailouts for the AI bubble when it pops

## What They Want

1. **Emergency powers** - Use the Iran war crisis to suspend elections and consolidate power
2. **AI surveillance state** - Palantir-style governance replacing democratic institutions
3. **Purge the old elite** - Replace Wall Street's financial extraction with tech's data extraction
4. **The Technate** - Aligned with Trump's vision of a self-sufficient, tech-governed North America

## Versus Wall Street

| | Old Elite (Wall Street) | Counter-Elite (Silicon Valley) |
|---|---|---|
| Party | Democrats | Republicans/MAGA |
| Bubble | Private credit ($2T) | AI |
| Strategy | War fails -> blame Trump -> win 2028 | War -> emergency powers -> suspend elections |
| World view | Maintain global financial empire | Build domestic tech-governed fortress |

Both factions are using the Iran war as a weapon in their domestic fight. Neither cares about the war's strategic outcome ([[law-of-proximity]]).

## Connection to Pax Judaica

Silicon Valley's surveillance technology is also the infrastructure for [[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica]]. Peter Thiel (Palantir) argued liberalism must yield to theocracy. The AI governance tools being built for domestic use can also serve Israel's vision of global AI-surveillance government.
