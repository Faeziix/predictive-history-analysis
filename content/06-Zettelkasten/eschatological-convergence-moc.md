# Eschatological Convergence

One of Professor Jiang's most distinctive arguments: the end-times prophecies of multiple world religions are converging on the same geography (Jerusalem) and the same timeframe. When powerful actors *believe* in prophecy and have the *means* to act on it, prophecy becomes self-fulfilling.

This is the [[law-of-eschatological-convergence]] in action.

## The Converging Prophecies

### [[jewish-eschatology]]
- Rebuilding the [[third-temple-prophecy|Third Temple]] on Temple Mount
- The coming of Mashiach (Messiah)
- Ingathering of exiles to Israel
- [[chabad-lubavitch]] movement's active pursuit of these goals

### [[christian-zionist-eschatology]]
- Third Temple as prerequisite for Second Coming of Christ
- Battle of Armageddon in the Holy Land
- Rapture theology driving US evangelical support for Israel
- Massive political influence on US foreign policy

### [[islamic-eschatology]]
- Defense of Al-Aqsa Mosque as religious duty
- The Mahdi's appearance during a time of great conflict
- Jerusalem (Al-Quds) as central to end-times narrative
- Iran's Shia eschatology specifically tied to resistance against injustice

### [[russian-orthodox-eschatology]]
- Moscow as the [[third-rome-concept|Third Rome]] (after Rome and Constantinople fell)
- Russia as defender of true Christianity
- Civilizational mission driving territorial ambition
- Theological justification for Ukraine conflict

## Why This Matters for Prediction

The insight isn't that prophecies are *true* - it's that they're *operative*. When the leaders of nuclear-armed states make decisions through eschatological frameworks, the prophecies shape reality whether or not they reflect it.

- Israel's leadership includes actors who genuinely want the [[third-temple-prophecy|Third Temple]]
- US evangelical base (core Trump constituency) supports Israel for eschatological reasons ([[christian-zionist-eschatology]])
- Iran's revolutionary ideology has eschatological dimensions ([[islamic-eschatology]])
- Putin frames Russia's mission in [[russian-orthodox-eschatology|Orthodox eschatological]] terms

The convergence point is Jerusalem. The trigger is the US-Iran war. The accelerant is that every major actor has religious reasons to escalate rather than compromise.
