# Russia

A massive resource fortress serving as the opposing pole to the Western financial order. In Jiang's framework, Russia's behavior is driven not by conventional geopolitics but by [[russian-orthodox-eschatology]] - the belief that Moscow is the [[third-rome-concept|Third Rome]], the last defender of true Christianity.

## Role in the Framework

Russia is one of the three predicted post-collapse power blocs (alongside the [[technate]] and [[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica]]). Its current actions are preparation for surviving and thriving after the global system collapses.

## Strategy: Deliberate Isolation

Following Aleksandr Dugin's framework, Russia is intentionally:

1. **Militarizing its economy.** The Ukraine war isn't about Ukraine - it's about transforming Russia into a total war economy that can survive without Western integration.

2. **Isolating from Western finance.** Sanctions and asset freezes (intended as punishment) actually serve Russia's goal of decoupling from a financial system it expects to collapse.

3. **Unifying through conflict.** The war spiritually unifies the population around Orthodox identity and civilizational mission, building the cohesion needed for the coming period of global chaos.

## Relationships

- **Allied with:** Iran (targeting intelligence, drone technology sharing)
- **Trading with:** China (energy, alternative payment systems)
- **At war with:** Ukraine, NATO (by proxy)
- **Future partner:** Germany (post-EU collapse), Iran
- **Eschatological adversary:** [[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica]] (the "Anti-Christ" AI state in Orthodox framing)

## Predicted Fate

1. Wins in Ukraine (though slowly - war of attrition)
2. Destroys NATO as a credible military alliance
3. Survives the global economic collapse because it already decoupled
4. Unites the Orthodox world under the Third Rome banner
5. Anchors a new Eurasian bloc with vast agricultural and energy resources
6. Eventually faces [[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica]] in the prophesied War of Gog and Magog

## Key Insight

**Russia isn't reacting to events - it's preparing for a collapse it considers inevitable.** What looks like aggression (Ukraine) or foolishness (isolation from the West) is actually positioning. If the global system collapses as Jiang predicts, Russia emerges as one of the few pre-adapted survivors.
