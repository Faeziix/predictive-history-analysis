# The Ethnic Cleansing of Palestine

Ilan Pappe's *The Ethnic Cleansing of Palestine* (2006) documents the systematic expulsion of approximately 800,000 Palestinians between late 1947 and mid-1949. Drawing on declassified Israeli military archives, Ben-Gurion's personal diary, and Palestinian oral histories, Pappe argues that the Nakba was not a byproduct of war but a premeditated campaign of ethnic cleansing planned in secret and executed with precision. These notes break the book's argument into atomic concepts, tracing the planning, execution, and aftermath of the Nakba.

## Core Narrative

- [[nakba-1948]] - The ethnic cleansing event itself: ~800,000 expelled, 531 villages destroyed, 11 urban neighborhoods emptied
- [[plan-dalet]] - The master blueprint finalized on March 10, 1948 that ordered the systematic expulsion
- [[village-files]] - The intelligence dossiers on every Arab village that made village-by-village cleansing operationally possible
- [[the-consultancy]] - Ben-Gurion's secret planning body that met at the Red House in Tel-Aviv
- [[david-ben-gurion]] - The architect who maintained a private commitment to "transfer" for decades while publicly accepting coexistence

## Context and Causes

- [[british-mandate-palestine]] - The colonial framework that incubated the Zionist project while suppressing Palestinian resistance
- [[1936-arab-revolt]] - The peasant uprising whose brutal suppression disarmed Palestinians before the decisive confrontation
- [[zionist-economic-colonization]] - JNF land purchases from absentee landlords, forced tenant evictions, and the "Jewish labor only" exclusion policy
- [[un-resolution-181]] - The partition plan that gave 56% of the land to 33% of the population and triggered the timetable for cleansing
- [[palestinian-elite-betrayal]] - How notable families sold land to Zionists while preaching resistance, leaving the peasantry without genuine leadership

## Enabling Factors

- [[zionist-paramilitary-organizations]] - The Haganah, Irgun, Stern Gang, and Palmach: their ideologies, Nazi connections, and convergence on ethnic cleansing
- [[jordan-zionist-collusion]] - The secret deal with King Abdullah that neutralized the strongest Arab army and enabled the cleansing
- [[arab-armies-phony-war]] - How all Arab armies were too weak, compromised, or treacherous to stop the ethnic cleansing

## Execution

- [[operation-nachshon]] - The first Plan Dalet operation, clearing the Tel Aviv-Jerusalem corridor and establishing the template
- [[deir-yassin-massacre]] - The emblematic massacre that weaponized terror as a tool of ethnic cleansing
- [[urbicide-of-palestine]] - The systematic depopulation of Palestine's major cities: Haifa, Jaffa, Tiberias, Safad, Acre, Lydda, Ramle
- [[tantura-massacre]] - The beach executions using village file kill lists, and the institutional suppression of the truth
- [[operation-hiram-completing-the-job]] - The final phase: Dawaymeh, Safsaf, and Sa'sa massacres, the Galilee sweep, and mopping-up operations
- [[negev-bedouin-expulsion]] - The eleven-year campaign (1948-1959) against 90,000 Bedouin across 96 tribes
