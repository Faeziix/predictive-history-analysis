# European Union

Caught between US demands and its own survival instincts. In the [[price-hierarchy]], Europe occupies the "knowledge economy" tier - generating significant value through engineering and IP but fatally dependent on external energy.

## Vulnerabilities

- **No domestic energy.** Dependent on Russian gas (now severed) and Middle Eastern oil (via [[strait-of-hormuz-chokepoint]])
- **Demographic collapse.** Aging populations with insufficient replacement
- **NATO dependency.** Military capacity outsourced to the US
- **De-industrialization.** Nordstream destruction accelerated the loss of competitive manufacturing (especially Germany)

## Predicted Fate

1. NATO collapses as the US retreats from global commitments
2. Energy crisis from Strait of Hormuz closure devastates economies
3. Massive migration from the Global South overwhelms social systems
4. De-industrialization continues without cheap Russian energy
5. Eventually fragments - Germany may partner with Russia, southern Europe faces worst outcomes
