# Geopolitical Actors

The Predictive History framework tracks actors at multiple levels: nations, transnational institutions, sub-state groups, and individuals. Each actor has a game they're playing, and understanding those games - and how they nest inside each other - is the point of [[universal-law-of-game-theory]].

## Nations

### The Great Powers
- [[united-states-actor]] - The declining empire. Split between the extractive establishment and Trump's [[technate]] project.
- [[iran-actor]] - The asymmetric power. Geographic and strategic advantages that make it unbeatable in a conventional war.
- [[israel-actor]] - Auditioning for empire manager. Proving ruthless capability through Gaza, Lebanon, and intelligence operations.
- [[russia-actor]] - The Third Rome aspirant. Orthodox eschatology driving geopolitical behavior.
- [[china-actor]] - The patient rival. Benefits from every US misstep without directly confronting.

### Regional Players
- [[gcc-states-actor]] - Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar. Vulnerable oil economies dependent on US security guarantees that are evaporating.
- [[japan-actor]] - The canary in the coal mine for demographic and economic collapse.
- [[european-union-actor]] - Caught between US demands and its own survival instincts.

## Transnational Forces
- [[transnational-capital]] - The layer above nation-states. Families, funds, and networks that operate beyond democratic accountability.
- [[rothschild-network]] - The archetypal dynasty: cross-border credit networks funding Zionism from the [[balfour-declaration]] to today.
- [[military-industrial-complex]] - Not a defense system but a [[engineered-boom-bust-cycles|wealth extraction mechanism]] disguised as one.
- [[neoconservative-movement]] - Former Trotskyites who captured US foreign policy for Israeli objectives. Authors of the [[clean-break-memo]].
- [[intelligence-networks]] - Mossad, CIA, and others as enforcement arms of [[transnational-capital]]. See [[CLAIM-013-epstein-intel-networks]].
- [[silicon-valley-actor]] - The surveillance infrastructure builders. Key to [[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica's]] AI governance vision.
- [[wall-street-actor]] - The financial machinery behind [[engineered-boom-bust-cycles]].

## Historical Figures
- [[theodor-herzl]] - Father of political Zionism. Built the institutional architecture that runs Washington lobbying to this day.

## Ideological Blocs
- [[christian-nationalism]] - The domestic ideology driving the [[technate]] vision.
- [[chabad-lubavitch]] - The eschatological movement connected to [[third-temple-prophecy]].
- [[russian-orthodox-eschatology]] - The "Third Rome" doctrine driving Russian expansionism.
