# Noahide Laws

**Core idea:** In hardline Zionist theology, when the Messiah arrives, non-Jews will be governed by the Seven Noahide Laws - a set of universal commandments for gentiles. Christians who refuse to submit will be "destroyed." This is the hidden endgame that American Christian Zionists are funding without understanding: the theology they're accelerating views Christianity itself as a false religion to be eradicated.

## What They Are

The Seven Noahide Laws are biblical commandments considered binding on all of humanity (not just Jews):

1. Do not worship idols
2. Do not blaspheme God
3. Do not murder
4. Do not steal
5. Do not commit sexual immorality
6. Do not eat flesh from a living animal
7. Establish courts of justice
