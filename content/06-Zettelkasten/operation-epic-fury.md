# Operation Epic Fury

The name given to the US military campaign to decapitate Iranian leadership. In Jiang's framework, it's the trigger event that sets the entire [[predicted-cascade-moc|predicted cascade]] in motion.

## The Plan vs Reality

**The Plan:** A 4-day operation using aerial bombardment and decapitation strikes to assassinate Iranian leaders (including Khamenei) and force quick surrender. Based on the assumption that removing leadership collapses resistance.

**The Reality (per Jiang):**
- Iran operates on Shia martyrdom theology ([[islamic-eschatology]]). Assassinating leaders doesn't break resolve - it *strengthens* it
- The assassination triggers unified global Jihad, not surrender
- Iran retaliates by targeting [[gcc-states-actor|GCC]] infrastructure and closing the [[strait-of-hormuz-chokepoint]]
- The "4-day operation" becomes a years-long quagmire

## The Anti-Powell Doctrine

The Substack "Countdown to Iran" notes that the operation violates every principle of the Powell Doctrine:
- **No clear interest** (beyond eschatological)
- **No limited objectives** (regime change is unlimited)
- **No overwhelming force** (against a prepared asymmetric opponent)
- **No public support** (70% opposed)

This total inversion of rational military strategy signals, in Jiang's view, that the war's purpose is *not* military victory. It's eschatological fulfillment.
