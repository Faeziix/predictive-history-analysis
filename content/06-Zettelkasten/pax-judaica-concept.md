# Pax Judaica

**Core idea:** The predicted replacement for Pax Americana. An AI-surveillance one-world government headquartered in Jerusalem, controlled by [[israel-actor|Israel]] and backed by relocated [[transnational-capital]].

## What It Is

Pax Judaica is the predicted end state where:

- Israel replaces the US as the Middle Eastern hegemon
- CENTCOM is absorbed into Israeli command structure
- GCC states become Israeli client states ([[CLAIM-011-israel-absorbs-gcc]])
- Jerusalem becomes the capital of a global AI-surveillance governance system
- Transnational capital relocates from New York/London to Israel
- The India-Middle East-Europe trade corridor is controlled by Israel

## How It Happens

1. **The Audition.** Israel demonstrates ruthless capability through Gaza, Lebanon, and Mossad operations. It proves to [[transnational-capital]] that it can control the region more effectively and cheaply than the bloated US military.

2. **The Crash.** The US-Iran war ([[CLAIM-001-us-iran-war]]) destroys American credibility and military prestige. The global elite recognize the American host is dying.

3. **Capital Flight.** Transnational capital engineers the popping of US bubbles (private credit, AI), extracts wealth, and relocates trillions to Israel.

4. **Territorial Expansion.** During the fog of war, Israel executes the Greater Israel Project - expanding from Egypt to Iraq. Destroyed GCC states are absorbed.

5. **The Third Temple.** Al-Aqsa Mosque is destroyed ([[CLAIM-005-al-aqsa-third-temple]]), blamed on an Iranian missile. The [[third-temple-prophecy|Third Temple]] is built. This is both a religious culmination and a political statement.

6. **AI Governance.** Using [[silicon-valley-actor|Silicon Valley]] technology (Palantir, etc.), Israel builds the surveillance infrastructure for global governance. Peter Thiel's argument that "liberalism must yield to theocracy" provides the ideological frame.

## The Eschatological Dimension

In Jiang's framework, this isn't just geopolitics - it's the [[law-of-eschatological-convergence]] reaching its endpoint:

- **Jewish eschatology:** Mashiach comes when the Third Temple is built
- **Christian Zionist eschatology:** The Third Temple triggers the Second Coming
- **Islamic eschatology:** The destruction of Al-Aqsa triggers the War of Gog and Magog
- **Secular eschatology:** The AI Singularity / "end of history"

All scripts converge on Jerusalem. Pax Judaica is where religion and technology merge.

## Criticism Within the Framework

Even within Jiang's framework, Pax Judaica faces threats:
- [[russia-actor|Russia]] and Iran represent the War of Gog and Magog opposition
- Trump has his own plans ([[technate]]) that diverge from the script
- The [[law-of-the-jungle]] means Israel eventually must accommodate Iran as an equal
