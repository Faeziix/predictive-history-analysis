# Israel

In Jiang's framework, Israel isn't just a nation defending itself - it's actively "auditioning" for [[transnational-capital]] to replace the United States as the next global empire manager. Everything Israel does - Gaza, Lebanon, intelligence operations - is a demonstration of capability to potential backers.

## Role in the Framework

Historically, Israel has been the Anglo-American "pitbull" in the Middle East - a mercenary state executing Western interests. But the US is declining, and Israel is positioning itself as the successor:

1. **The Audition.** Gaza and Lebanon operations demonstrate ruthless capability at low cost. Compare Israel's Mossad operations (pager attacks, targeted assassinations) with the US military's multi-billion-dollar failures. Israel proves it can control the region more effectively and cheaply than American blunt force.

2. **The Greater Israel Project.** Territorial expansion from Egypt to Iraq, controlling the India-Middle East-Europe trade corridor, energy resources, and data center infrastructure.

3. **[[pax-judaica-concept|Pax Judaica]].** The end state: an AI-surveillance one-world government headquartered in Jerusalem, backed by relocated [[transnational-capital]].

## Motivations

- Execute the Greater Israel Project
- Absorb destroyed [[gcc-states-actor|GCC states]] ([[CLAIM-011-israel-absorbs-gcc]])
- Become the epicenter of transnational capital and AI surveillance
- Build the [[third-temple-prophecy|Third Temple]]
- Netanyahu personally: maintain state of emergency to avoid prison for corruption

## The Hidden Strategy

Israel doesn't benefit from a quick US victory in Iran. It benefits from a *protracted* conflict that:
- Fractures Iran's capacity to challenge Greater Israel
- Plunges America into civil war (removing the US as a competitor)
- Unites Muslims against Israel (triggering the prophesied War of Gog and Magog, which Israel believes it will win)
- Creates chaos that justifies emergency expansion

The [[CLAIM-005-al-aqsa-third-temple|destruction of Al-Aqsa]] during the fog of war (blamed on an Iranian missile) is the religious trigger. The economic restructuring (capital fleeing US to Israel) is the financial trigger.

## Relationships

- **Outward ally, hidden rival:** United States
- **Future partner:** Iran (per [[law-of-the-jungle]])
- **Targets:** GCC states (for absorption)
- **Sponsors:** [[transnational-capital]] (preparing to relocate)
- **Religious allies:** [[chabad-lubavitch]], Christian Zionists
- **Tools:** Mossad, [[silicon-valley-actor]] (Palantir, AI infrastructure)

## Predicted Fate

Israel replaces America as Middle Eastern hegemon. Absorbs GCC states. Controls trade corridors and energy. Becomes headquarters for a global AI-surveillance governance system. Eventually partners with Iran to partition the Middle East.
