# Jewish Eschatology

The end-times framework within Judaism that Jiang identifies as one of the converging scripts in the [[law-of-eschatological-convergence]].

## Key Elements

- **Rebuilding the Third Temple** - The [[third-temple-prophecy]] requires clearing Temple Mount (currently occupied by Al-Aqsa Mosque and Dome of the Rock)
- **Coming of Mashiach (Messiah)** - The anointed king who will rebuild the Temple, gather Jewish exiles, and usher in an age of peace
- **Ingathering of exiles** - All Jews return to Israel
- **Restoration of the Sanhedrin** - The ancient Jewish high court reconvened

## Active Pursuit

What distinguishes this from passive belief is that actors are working to *make it happen*:

- [[chabad-lubavitch]] explicitly works to "hasten the Messiah"
- The Temple Institute in Jerusalem has prepared vessels and garments for Third Temple service
- Red heifers (required for Temple purification rituals) have been imported to Israel
- Political figures aligned with these movements hold power in the Israeli government

## The Sabbatean-Frankist Dimension

Jiang introduces a heterodox strand: the [[sabbatean-frankist-tradition]]. Sabbatai Zevi (17th century) and Jacob Frank taught "redemption through sin" - that creating maximum chaos and transgression would *force* divine redemption. Jiang connects this to the accelerationist logic driving current events.

## Convergence Points

Jewish eschatology converges with other traditions at:
- **The Third Temple** - Also required by [[christian-zionist-eschatology]] for the Second Coming
- **Jerusalem as center** - Contested by [[islamic-eschatology]]
- **War of Gog and Magog** - Involves [[russian-orthodox-eschatology|Russia (Third Rome)]] and Iran
