# Israel's AI Surveillance Systems and Global Technology Export

## Summary
Israel has deployed advanced AI systems in Gaza: Lavender (targeting 37,000 Palestinians), Gospel (automated bombing targets), Blue Wolf/Red Wolf (facial recognition surveillance). These "battle-tested" technologies are marketed globally, making Israel one of the world's largest arms exporters relative to size. This supports the technological dominance aspect of the "Pax Judaica" thesis.
