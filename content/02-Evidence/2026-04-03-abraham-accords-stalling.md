# Abraham Accords Stalling — Saudi Arabia Refuses Normalization

## Summary
Saudi Arabia has refused formal normalization despite US pressure, F-35 deals, and investment offers. 99% of Saudi respondents oppose normalization. The war in Gaza and Iran has further dampened prospects. Kazakhstan joined but major GCC expansion has stalled.

## Key Quotes
> MBS: "The Kingdom will not establish diplomatic relations with Israel without [independent Palestinian state]"
> "99% of Saudi respondents answered that establishing normal relations with Israel would be a negative step"
> ADL expressed "grave concern" over antisemitic discourse in Saudi Arabia

INSS (Israeli), Washington Institute, and Middle East Eye provide multi-perspective coverage. High credibility for diplomatic analysis.
