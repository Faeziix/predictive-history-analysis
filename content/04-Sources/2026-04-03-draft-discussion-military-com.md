# Could There Be a Military Draft? Trump Administration Says It's 'On Table'

## Summary
White House Press Secretary Karoline Leavitt refused to rule out a military draft, stating Trump "wisely keeps his options on the table." Congress approved automatic Selective Service registration in the 2026 NDAA. ~75% of Americans oppose reinstating the draft per Pew Research.

## Key Data Points
- White House says draft is "on the table"
- 2026 NDAA authorizes automatic involuntary Selective Service registration starting Dec 18, 2026
- ~75% of Americans oppose draft (Pew Research)
- Former Rep. Marjorie Taylor Greene strongly opposed: "Not my son, over my dead body"
- No current plan for draft or ground troops officially announced
- Draft speculation surged following Iran war escalation
