# US Ground Forces Arrive in Middle East as Iran Conflict Escalates

## Summary
Military.com reports on the deployment of US ground forces to the Middle East, including 2,500 Marines aboard USS Tripoli and elements of the 82nd Airborne Division. Pentagon preparing for weeks of limited ground operations in Iran including potential raids on Kharg Island.

## Key Data Points
- 2,500 Marines and sailors deployed aboard USS Tripoli
- 82nd Airborne Division elements deployed to region
- Pentagon preparing for "weeks of limited ground operations"
- Potential seizure of Kharg Island (key Iranian oil export hub)
- Plans fall short of full invasion but include raids by SOF and infantry
- Up to 17,000 troops may be deployed near Iran

## Bias Assessment
Military.com is a trusted source for US military news with strong Pentagon sources. Generally factual reporting with pro-military editorial lean.
