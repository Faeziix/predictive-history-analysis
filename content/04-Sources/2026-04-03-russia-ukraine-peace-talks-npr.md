# The UK and France Would Install Military Hubs in Ukraine as Part of a Peace Plan

## Summary
Major progress in Ukraine peace talks at January 2026 Paris summit. UK and France pledged to deploy forces and establish military hubs in Ukraine if ceasefire reached. US backs security guarantees including truce monitoring mechanism. Deal described as 90% complete but Russia stalling.

## Key Data Points
- Paris summit January 2026 yielded "significant progress"
- UK and France to establish "military hubs" across Ukraine
- US to lead ceasefire monitoring mechanism (drones, sensors, satellites - not troops)
- European, US, Ukrainian officials say deal is 90% complete
- Russia opposes NATO troop deployment on Ukrainian soil
- US gave June 2026 deadline for peace deal
- Russia demanding Ukraine withdraw from Donbas (Kyiv refuses)
- Putin "not showing he is ready for peace" per UK PM
