# How Iran Is Blocking the Strait of Hormuz Shipping Route Despite Trump's Threats

## Summary
Bloomberg reports on Iran's partial blockade of the Strait of Hormuz, with 70% reduction in traffic, ~2,000 ships stranded, and 21 confirmed attacks on merchant ships since March 4, 2026. Oil prices have surged ~50% from pre-war levels.

## Key Data Points
- Iran declared Strait "closed" starting March 4, 2026
- 70% reduction in shipping traffic
- ~2,000 ships stranded in the region (per IMO)
- 21 confirmed attacks on merchant ships as of March 12
- Oil prices up ~50%, from ~$70 to ~$108/barrel by March 26
- $200/barrel scenario being considered by analysts
- ~20% of global oil supply transits the Strait daily

## Bias Assessment
Bloomberg is a premier financial news outlet with high credibility for economic and market reporting. Data-driven approach with strong sourcing.
