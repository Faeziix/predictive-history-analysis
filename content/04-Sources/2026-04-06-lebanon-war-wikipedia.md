# 2026 Lebanon War

## Summary
Israel launched ground invasion of southern Lebanon on March 16, 2026, aiming to occupy territory up to the Litani River. Hezbollah fired 1,800+ rockets into Israel. 1,001+ killed, 2,584 wounded in Lebanon. 1M+ Lebanese displaced (1/6 of population). Lebanese cabinet banned Hezbollah military wing. Iran conditioned ceasefire on ending Lebanon war.

## Key Data Points
- Israeli ground invasion began March 16
- Israel plans to occupy up to Litani River
- Hezbollah fired 1,800+ rockets into Israel during first month
- 1,001+ killed, 2,584+ wounded by Israeli strikes in Lebanon
- 1M+ Lebanese displaced (1/6 of population)
- Lebanese cabinet voted to ban Hezbollah's military/security apparatus
- Iranian ambassador blocked from leaving by Shiite parties
- 150 rockets from Lebanon during Passover (April 1-2)
- Iran conditioned any ceasefire on ending Lebanon war
