# The Gulf Monarchies Are Caught Between Iran's Desperation and the US's Recklessness

## Summary
Iran attacked ALL GCC states within 48 hours of the war starting. UAE suffered the brunt with hundreds of drones/missiles. Saudi Arabia's largest oil refinery put out of commission. Kuwait airport struck by drone fire. Dubai luxury hotels damaged. Qatar's helium supply (1/3 of global) cut off. GCC unity strengthened but their post-oil economic model (tourism, data centers, logistics) is threatened.

## Key Data Points
- Iran targeted all GCC states within 48 hours of war start
- UAE suffered hundreds of drones and missiles
- Saudi Arabia's largest oil refinery put out of commission
- Kuwait International Airport struck by drone fire (again on April 1)
- Fires near luxury hotels in Dubai
- Qatar's helium supply (1/3 of world's) cut off from market
- Casualties: Bahrain 3, Kuwait 8, Oman 3, Saudi 2, UAE 8
- Saudi Arabia now permitting US forces to operate from its territory
- 1M+ Lebanese displaced by Israeli invasion
