# Israel Cancels Friday Prayers at Al-Aqsa Mosque Amid Iran Conflict

## Summary
Israel cancelled Friday prayers at Al-Aqsa during Ramadan amid the Iran war. Eight Arab/Islamic countries condemned the closure, which lasted 12+ consecutive days. Ben-Gvir previously stated he would build a synagogue at the Al-Aqsa compound.

## Key Data Points
- Friday prayers cancelled at Al-Aqsa during Ramadan
- Closure lasted 12+ consecutive days
- 8 countries condemned: Qatar, Jordan, Indonesia, Turkey, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, UAE
- Minister Ben-Gvir expressed desire to build synagogue at Al-Aqsa compound
- Defence Minister Gallant called Ben-Gvir's actions "dangerous, unnecessary, irresponsible"
- Iranian missiles and debris landed in Jerusalem's Old City
- Tucker Carlson warned of Israeli plot to destroy Al-Aqsa and blame Iran
