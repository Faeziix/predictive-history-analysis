# Saudi Arabia's New Approach to Israel and the Normalization Process

## Summary
Despite Trump administration efforts, Saudi Arabia remains uncommitted to formal normalization with Israel. MBS refused to announce formal ties during Nov 2025 US visit despite F-35 deals. 99% of Saudi respondents view normalization negatively. Kazakhstan joined Abraham Accords in early 2026.

## Key Data Points
- Saudi Arabia refused formal normalization despite F-35 and investment deals
- MBS: "no diplomatic relations with Israel without independent Palestinian state"
- 99% of Saudi respondents opposed normalization (Aug 2025 survey)
- Kazakhstan joined Abraham Accords in early 2026 (first Central Asian nation)
- Trump sought to expand Accords to Syria, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia (July 2025)
- Netanyahu expanded Accords by recognizing Somaliland (Dec 2025)
- ADL expressed "grave concern" over antisemitic discourse in Saudi Arabia (Jan 2026)

## Bias Assessment
INSS is an Israeli think tank. Analysis reflects Israeli strategic perspective. Data on Saudi public opinion sourced from independent polling.
