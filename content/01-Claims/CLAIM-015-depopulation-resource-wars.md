# Massive Global Depopulation and Resource Wars

## Original Claim

> There will be massive global depopulation through resource wars, widespread famine in the global south, massive migration destabilizing North America and Europe, and nations may return to slavery as a primary energy source.

## Context
Professor Jiang sees depopulation as an inevitable consequence of the collapse of globalized agriculture and energy systems. Without cheap oil, modern farming cannot sustain current population levels. He also predicts aging populations in developed nations will face crises as migration pressures overwhelm social systems.
